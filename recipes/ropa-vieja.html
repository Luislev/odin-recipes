Ropa vieja

Description:

This recipe is from the panamanian gastronomy, this dish consist about a delectable beef stew with some vegetables, soaked up with sauce and it can be eaten with rice in the lunch, or in the dinner with some "patacones" (fried plantain slices)

Making this recipe will take you about an hour to be prepared, and it will be enough for 4 people.

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg (2.2lbs) of flank steak
  • 2 medium onions
  • tomato paste
  • oil of your preference (for this recipe we will use olive oil)
  • 2 bell tomatoes
  • 4 cilantro (coriander) sheets
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • salt and pepper

Steps:

  1. Prepare the flank steak, take it to a pot with enoguh water and cook it until the meat gets tender.
  2. Take out the meat and wait for the meat to cool down to shred it. Important: Keep the beef stock in the pot, we're gonna use it later.
  3. Cut the onions and peppers in julienne, the garlic in ittle pieces and the tomatoes in cubes.
  4. Heat a pan with some oil and put the vegetables already cutted into the pan.
  5. Add tomato paste for the sauce.
  6. Incorporate the shredded beef and the stock in the pan.
  7. Add the cilantro, salt and pepper and cook everything at low heat for 30 minutes till the sauce gets reduced.
  8. When the sauce is almost reduced, turn off the heat and wait some minutes to cool down a bit.
