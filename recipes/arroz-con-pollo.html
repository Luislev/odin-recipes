Arroz con pollo (panamanian style)

Description:

This repice is very emblematic and so popular for panamanians, we eat this food at lunch, especially in parties, birthdays, or in special days like new year or christmas. Arroz con pollo (rice with chicken in english) is traditionally a dish with cooked and soft yellow rice, combined with vegetables and little chunks of chicken.

This recipe will take you about 1hr - 1hr 30 minutes to finish it and its enough for 6 portions with this recipe.
